# User Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: March 17, 2026

## How to Request Data Deletion

This application does not store any personal data on external servers. 
All data accessed through the Meta API is processed in real time and 
never saved outside of your local device.

However, if you want to ensure all access is revoked, follow these steps:

### Step 1 — Remove App Permissions on Facebook
1. Go to your [Facebook Settings](https://www.facebook.com/settings)
2. Click on **"Security and Login"** or **"Apps and Websites"**
3. Find this app in the list
4. Click **"Remove"** to revoke all permissions and delete any associated data

### Step 2 — Revoke Access Tokens
1. Go to [Meta for Developers](https://developers.facebook.com)
2. Navigate to **"Tools" → "Access Token Debugger"**
3. Paste your token and click **"Revoke Token"**

### Step 3 — Contact Us
If you need further assistance or confirmation of data deletion, contact:

**Email:** luixfraga@gmail.com

We will respond within 30 days of receiving your request.

## What Data We Access
- Ad campaign data from your Meta Business account
- Ad metrics and insights
- No personal data from third-party users is ever collected or stored

## Confirmation
Once permissions are removed from Facebook Settings, all access is 
immediately revoked. You will receive email confirmation upon request.
